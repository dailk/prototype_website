There have been many famous soccer players throughout history. They have been national heroes and won the World Cup for their country. I will list several great players that I think are amazing.

Clint Dempsey

He was born in Nacogdoches, Texas and lived in a trailer park. He played with Mexican immigrants and later on joined the Dallas Texans. He is the only U.S player that played and scored in three World Cups. In 2014 he scored the 5th fastest goal in World Cup history in 29 seconds. He plays in the MLS (Major League Soccer) for the Seattle Sounders as a forward/midfield.

Adelino Gonsalves

He played in many different professional U.S teams and holds the record for playing on the most National Challenge Cup teams that won. In 1930 and 1934 he played in the World Cup as a forward. He was one of the first to be put in the U.S National Soccer Hall of Fame in 1950.
